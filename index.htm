Profile & Capacity Statement

Who we are
Founded in 2009, Agribusiness & Environmental Focus for Africa (AEFA) works with communities and partners to improve livelihoods through Sustainable Community Empowerment Initiatives.

Vision

To see a self‑sustaining society.

Mission

To empower vulnerable groups with an aim of achieving livelihood‑related MDGs at community level.

Objectives

  1. Develop community working groups as a tool for mobilization, sensitization and awareness creation.
  2. Empower communities towards attaining self‑sustenance.
  3. Network with other actors in facilitating and implementing community development projects.

Area coverage: Machakos, Makueni & Kitui
Target population: Smallholder farmers

Our approach

AEFA's strategy for sustainable livelihoods is guided by three complementary approaches.

1. Empowering youth & women

We build capacities, information access and leadership for women and youth so they can act as change agents in their households and communities.

2. Grassroots mobilization

We mobilize communities into self‑help groups and use participatory decision‑making to strengthen leadership, access to credit, extension services and value chains.

3. Strategic partnerships

We partner with county governments, CBOs, universities, research institutions and the private sector to scale skills, research and market access.

Key interventions

  • Capacity development: SHG formation (20–40 members), leadership, bookkeeping and enterprise skills.
  • Value addition & technology transfer: processing, packaging and market positioning.
  • Knowledge management & market linkages.
  • Agro‑enterprise stimulation and revitalization of agricultural production.
  • Prevention of post‑harvest losses and improving competitiveness.

Our Projects

Climate Smart Agriculture

We promote resilient farming practices, agri‑entrepreneurship for women and youth, and value addition while protecting the environment. Interventions include training on Good Agricultural Practices (GAP), drip irrigation kits, gunny bag kitchen gardens, water‑retaining pits and resource mobilization.

Activities: seedling distribution, GAP training, value addition, market linkages and resource mobilization.

Environment, Climate Change & Productive Landscapes

We implement biodiversity and tree‑growing initiatives, promote energy‑efficient cookstoves, and build capacity in agroforestry and sustainable forest‑based enterprises.

Youth & Women Empowerment

We deliver leadership & governance training, enterprise development, post‑harvest handling, branding, access to credit and market linkages to transform livelihoods.

Value Addition & Market Development

From processing to packaging, we help farmers increase shelf life and value of produce and link them to buyers to improve incomes.
