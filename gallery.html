Profile & Capacity Statement

Who we are: Founded in 2009, AEFA works with communities and partners to improve livelihoods through Sustainable Community Empowerment Initiatives.

Vision

To see a self-sustaining society.

Mission

To empower vulnerable groups to achieve livelihood-related MDGs at community level.

Objectives

  1. Develop community working groups for mobilization, sensitization and awareness.
  2. Empower communities towards self-sustenance.
  3. Network with other actors for community development projects.

Area coverage: Machakos, Makueni & Kitui
Target population: Smallholder farmers

Our Approach

1. Empowering youth & women

We build leadership and capacity for women and youth as key change agents.

2. Grassroots mobilization

We organize self-help groups and promote participatory decision-making for stronger communities.

3. Strategic partnerships

We partner with governments, CBOs, universities and research institutions to scale solutions.

Our Projects

Climate Smart Agriculture

Resilient farming, agri-entrepreneurship for women and youth, and value addition while protecting the environment.

Environment & Productive Landscapes

Tree planting, cookstoves, agroforestry and sustainable forest enterprises.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Leadership training, post-harvest handling, branding, credit and market linkages.

Value Addition & Market Development

Processing, packaging and linking farmers to markets.
