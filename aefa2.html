Empowering smallholder farmers towards sustainable, climate-resilient livelihoods

AEFA works with communities, county governments and research institutions across Machakos, Makueni and Kitui to transform smallholder agriculture into profitable, sustainable agribusiness through capacity development, climate smart interventions and market linkages.
